When is native WebVR support for Google Cardboard landing in release-channel browsers?

Using the WebVR Polyfill, WebVR sites can still be viewed with a Google Cardboard viewer on an iPhone 6 or better (for iOS) and Samsung Note 5 or better (for Android). Using the WebVR Polyfill, however, means there are no guarantees of stability nor performance.

Native support for WebVR has shipped in the latest release-channel version of {{ browsers.chrome_for_android.name }}. Any modern Android phone is supported, but it is recommended to use at least a Samsung Note 5 or better.
